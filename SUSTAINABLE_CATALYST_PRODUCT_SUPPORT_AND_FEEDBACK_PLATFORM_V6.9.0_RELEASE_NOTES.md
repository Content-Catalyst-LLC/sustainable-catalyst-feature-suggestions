# Sustainable Catalyst Product Support and Feedback Platform v6.9.0

## Quality Assurance, Analytics, and Support Intelligence

This release converts private case activity into governed operational evidence while keeping the public Support Center and private case record boundaries intact.

## Capabilities

- Portfolio, product, team, and queue analytics scopes
- Backlog age and pressure
- SLA-compliance evidence
- Reopen and escalation analysis
- Documentation-assisted resolution measurement
- Governed case-quality reviews across diagnosis, evidence, communication, resolution, documentation, and privacy
- Append-only quality findings and improvement actions
- Daily integrity-verifiable snapshots
- Metric time series
- Product-support pressure signals
- Minimum-cohort suppression
- Private JSON exports with SHA-256 integrity
- Agent Workspace quality panel
- Private REST API, FastAPI parity, and WP-CLI

## Boundaries

The release does not expose requester identity, private correspondence, attachment content, or individual customer records. Quality review does not automatically rank personnel, take punitive action, change case status, assign work, declare incidents, alter roadmaps, or publish findings.
