Product Support and Feedback Platform v6.10.0 — Institutional Workspaces and Access Governance

Place the release bundle and installer in ~/Downloads, then run:

cd ~/Downloads
chmod +x install_and_push_v6_10_0_macos.sh
./install_and_push_v6_10_0_macos.sh

Optional selection-only preflight:

SCFS_PREFLIGHT_ONLY=1 ./install_and_push_v6_10_0_macos.sh

Expected installer revision:
V6_10_0_CHECKSUM_SYNC_PARITY

The installer uses Python 3.13 or 3.12, installs validation dependencies, validates the packaged source, synchronizes the repository with rsync --checksum, verifies SHA-256 tree parity, validates again, commits, tags v6.10.0, and pushes without force.
