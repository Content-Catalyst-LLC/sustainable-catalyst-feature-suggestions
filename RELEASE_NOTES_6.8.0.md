# Product Support and Feedback Platform v6.8.0

## Email and Channel Operations

v6.8.0 adds authenticated support-email intake, case-thread matching, governed outbound email drafts, delivery and bounce evidence, least-privilege channel authorization, and Microsoft Teams troubleshooting handoffs.

Contact and Engagement remains authoritative for requester identity, email transport, secure attachment bytes, consent, and Teams scheduling. The help desk stores case-linked channel metadata, content hashes, message state, delivery evidence, and governed handoff references.

The release does not create cases, send customer email, close cases, expose a public inbound webhook, or schedule meetings automatically.
