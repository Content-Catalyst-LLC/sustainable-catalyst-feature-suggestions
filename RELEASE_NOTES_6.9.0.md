# Product Support and Feedback Platform v6.9.0

## Quality Assurance, Analytics, and Support Intelligence

v6.9.0 adds a private quality and analytics layer for help-desk operations. It measures backlog pressure, SLA performance, reopen and escalation rates, documentation-assisted resolution, governed case-quality reviews, trend evidence, and privacy-safe product-support pressure.

### Additive private tables

- `scfs_help_desk_quality_reviews`
- `scfs_help_desk_quality_findings`
- `scfs_help_desk_analytics_snapshots`
- `scfs_help_desk_metric_series`
- `scfs_help_desk_support_signals`
- `scfs_help_desk_quality_actions`

### Governance

Analytics require minimum privacy-safe cohorts. Requester identities, private message bodies, and attachment content are excluded. The platform does not create automatic personnel rankings, punitive actions, case transitions, incident declarations, roadmap changes, or public disclosures.
