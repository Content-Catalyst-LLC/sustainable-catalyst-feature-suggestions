How Feature Suggestions Work

The suggestion form is designed to turn loose feedback into a reviewable platform backlog.

  • Scope What to Suggest Modules, demos, repositories, documentation, article maps, accessibility fixes, and Research Librarian ideas.
  • Process How Suggestions Are Reviewed Submissions become private review records that can be organized, exported, and considered for development.
  • Limits Submission Boundaries Non-confidential feedback only; no sensitive, regulated, or proprietary information.
  • Form Submit a Suggestion Use the structured form to explain the feature, problem, audience, priority, and relevant page or repository.

Review Process

From Suggestion to Backlog

The form saves each submission as a private WordPress record, sends a notification, and supports CSV export. This makes suggestions reviewable instead of letting them disappear into a generic contact inbox.

Submit

A visitor explains the feature, the problem it solves, who benefits, and where it fits.

Review

The suggestion is stored privately for review, triage, and follow-up if permission is given.

Prioritize

Suggestions can be grouped by category, urgency, feasibility, platform fit, and reuse value.

Build

Strong suggestions may inform future pages, demos, plugins, repositories, or Research Librarian features.
