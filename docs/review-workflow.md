# Review Workflow

Suggested review path:

1. Open **Feature Suggestions** in WordPress admin.
2. Review new **Pending Review** submissions.
3. Classify by category, priority, and workflow status.
4. Add impact score and effort score.
5. Assign a roadmap area such as Research Library, Research Librarian, GitHub, Demo, Documentation, Accessibility, or Platform.
6. Add a GitHub issue URL when the suggestion becomes actionable.
7. Use internal notes for triage decisions, implementation concerns, or duplicate links.
8. Export CSV periodically for offline review or backlog planning.
9. Do not publish user-submitted contact details or private notes.

Built-in workflow statuses:

- New
- Reviewed
- Planned
- In progress
- Implemented
- Declined
- Duplicate
- Archived

Impact and effort scores use a 0–5 scale. A simple triage heuristic is:

```text
High impact + low effort = fast-track candidate
High impact + high effort = roadmap candidate
Low impact + low effort = polish/backlog candidate
Low impact + high effort = usually decline or defer
```
