Sustainable Catalyst Product Support and Feedback Platform v5.7.0
Support Analytics and Documentation Effectiveness

1. Download these files into ~/Downloads:
   - sustainable-catalyst-product-support-feedback-platform-v5.7.0-release-bundle.zip
   - install_and_push_v5_7_0_macos.sh

2. Run:

cd ~/Downloads
chmod +x install_and_push_v5_7_0_macos.sh
./install_and_push_v5_7_0_macos.sh

The installer accepts Python 3.12 or 3.13 and rejects Python 3.14. It creates a safety backup, validates the source, commits v5.7.0, tags v5.7.0, and pushes main.

After deployment, open WordPress Admin → Support & Feedback → Support Analytics.
