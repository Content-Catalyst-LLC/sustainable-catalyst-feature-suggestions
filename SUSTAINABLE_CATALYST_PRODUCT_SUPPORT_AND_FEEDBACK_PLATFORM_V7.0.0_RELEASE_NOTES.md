# Product Support and Feedback Platform v7.0.0

## Connected Help Desk and Support Operations Platform

v7.0.0 unifies the complete public-support and private help-desk stack into one governed operating platform. It adds cross-module health, privacy-safe case dossiers, end-to-end support journeys, command authorization plans, handoff contexts, daily operating snapshots, and integrity-verified reports.

The release remains additive. Existing public records, private cases, portal sessions, assignments, messages, evidence, service clocks, workflows, email records, analytics, institutional workspaces, integrations, and production-hardening evidence remain authoritative in their existing modules.

Connected commands are plans. Customer communication, case transitions, access grants, destructive privacy operations, external issue creation, and deployments still require an authorized human and execution through the authoritative module.

## Seven operating layers

1. Public Support Center
2. Customer Portal
3. Agent Operations
4. Knowledge Operations
5. Service Management
6. Product Intelligence
7. Institutional Integration
